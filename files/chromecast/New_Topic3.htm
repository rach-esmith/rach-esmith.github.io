Streaming from Google Chrome to Your TV

Now that you have prepared the Chromecast and set up your browser, you are fully equipped to start streaming, or in this case, “Casting” from your laptop. Unlike with a phone or a tablet, which only allow you to Cast from specific apps, while using your laptop you are able to stream anything you can view within your Google Chrome browser.

  1. Connect your laptop to the same Wi-Fi network as your Chromecast.
  2. Open Google Chrome and click on the Chromecast icon in the top right corner of your browser.
  3. Click on the name of your Chromecast under “Cast this tab to…”

    The page you currently have as your active tab appears on your TV.

    Note that you are not able to see your laptop’s cursor on the TV screen, but can click and interact with buttons and links the same way you normally would.
