Casting from a Phone or Tablet

Utilizing a Chromecast from your phone or tablet requires very little setup and is the most convenient way to enjoy the Chromecast’s features. There are lots of available apps that are compatible with the Chromecast. For a full list visit: google.com/chrome/devices/chromecast/apps.html

This guide demonstrates how to stream videos from YouTube. Once you know how to use Chromecast with this app, you should be ready to “Cast” from any Chromecast-ready app you like!

Casting from YouTube:

  1. Connect your phone or tablet to the same Wi-Fi network as your Chromecast.
  2. Open up the YouTube App. As long as you are on the correct Wi-Fi network, you should see the Chromecast logo in the top right of the app.
  3. Tap on the logo and select the name of the Chromecast device you want to connect to. The YouTube logo appears on your TV screen, and you can
    now search for a video to stream to your TV.
  4. Find the video you want to Cast, and then tap the play button. A pop up appears asking if you want to play it or add the video to a queue. Building up a queue is a great way to keep the videos going, and anyone on the same Wi-Fi as the Chromecast can contribute to it!
  5. Disconnect from the Chromecast at any time by tapping on the Comecast logo and choosing 'Disconnect.'

For help using your Chromecast on other compatible apps, check out the Google Chrome YouTube channel, which features a playlist of Chromecast guided tours to walk you through more apps and features you might not know about.
