Setting up Google Cast on Your Laptop

  1. Install the Google Chrome browser on your computer if you have not done so already.
  2. Open Chrome and click on the “Customize and Control Google Chrome” button in the top right of the browser. go down to “More tools” and hover over it. Click on “Extensions.”
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Extensions page and click “Get more extensions.” This takes you to the Chrome web store.
  4. Search the store for “Google Cast” and find the extension with the same name. The icon looks like this:
  5. Click on the button. A confirmation box appears asking if you would like to add Google Cast. Click “Add.” The extension is automatically downloaded and installed in your browser.
  6. There should now be an icon in the top right of your browser next to “Customize and Control Google Chrome” that looks like this: . Click on it. The first time you do so, Google Cast will ask you if you’d like to help improve the service by sharing your usage statistics. You may uncheck this box if you do not want to do so.
  7. Click “OK” to begin using Google Cast.
