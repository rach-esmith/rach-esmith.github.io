The projects contained within this page include all of the major assignments I have completed as part of the Professional Writing and Technical Communication (PWTC) program.

Mock Grant Proposal

As practice writing professional grant proposals, I was tasked with researching a non-profit and a grant-gifting foundation that they could potentially apply to. I then conducted extensive research in order to write a realistic and convincing proposal on a topic concerning the improvement and advancement of the non-profit.

Word 2013 Manual

The purpose of this project was to go through the process of creating a user guide for Microsoft Word 2013 targeted towards a specific audience. I decided on my target audience and, following user testing and research on Word's features, developed modules containing information that I believed would be most useful to them.

InDesign Guide

This PDF pamphlet was created in Adobe InDesign using content from the UMass Amherst English Undergraduate website. The goal of the project was to become confident with InDesign and its tools, in addition to working on our editorial skills by deciding what content from the original document would be included in our final guide and in what order.

RoboHelp Guide

This assignment provided practice in creating clear and concise instructions on a topic of our choice and knowledge on how the create guides using Adobe Robohelp. I researched and wrote the contet, then designed the layout and structured the information.
